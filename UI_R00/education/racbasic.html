    Question: "Why do you need this course?"

    Answer: When you look here, we guess you already have an image of BIM. So this course will provide you with the basics and from here you can keep researching for yourself or follow the next classes.

    Answer: (1) Understand why BIM is so hot and is the trending in civil engineering field!
