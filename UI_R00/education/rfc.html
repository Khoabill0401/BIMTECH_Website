    B. COURSE OUTLINES

    BIM is developping stronger and stronger everyday, even dominate over all other subjects, from forming ideas to design phase, from processing to construction phase, from operation to using and destruction phase.

    Along with this progress is the appearances from series of BIM tools with the technology changed day by day. It makes not only “rookies” but even some experts have the feeling of panic: "My knowledge at the moment can be applied until when?"

    This is the purpose of BIMTech course. As technology constantly changes daily, learning to use software would only helps us to face temporarily situation at the present day and then gradually following all new trending in the future.

    Come to BIMTech courses to equip yourself with solid foundation knowledge and fast-paced, modern research methods.

    Be the leader, do not step on the footprints in the shadow.
