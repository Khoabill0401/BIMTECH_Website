    • ALUMINUM FORMWORK DESIGN

    Design: BIMTech

    In the development and application of BIM technology, an add-in product developed by BIMTech which can be simulated rapidly and accurately with the Level Of Detail (LOD) 300 ÷ 400: " BIM Application for Aluminum Formwork ".

    With the technological development trend in the construction industry, the widespread adoption and utilization of aluminum formworks in construction will accelerate your project progress compared to conventional formworks method. By the other hand, skilled and experienced human resources are required.

    FACING WITH COMPLICATED AND HIGHLY ABSTRACT TASK, FIRST THING WE WOULD WHINK ABOUT IS EXPERIENCES

    WITH YOUR ACCUMULATED EXPERIENCES, YOU WOULD SEND YOUR DESIGN DRAWINGS WITH CONFIDENT BUT IS IT POSSIBLE TO BE CONSTRUCTED?

    WHY DON'T WE APPLY BIM FOR OUR PROCESS?

    WITH BIM YOU CAN HAVE:

    • The visualization of the "Product" you have just made. Based on that, you can easily adjust your "Product" for design optimization,well adapted to your current status in reality.

    • Precise quantities management in the design process.

    With a team of experienced and well BIM application of aluminum formwork designer, we meet all requirements of high accuracy aluminum formwork design such as:
    • New formwork design.
    • Formwork design with utilizing the amount of formworks in stock.
    • Manufacturing Drawings of aluminum formwork: Design, Manufacture, Construct.
    • Etc.

    Some projects we have done:

    • Lucky Palace

    • Garden Gate

    • Botonica

    • The Sun Avenue

    With the concept of optimal design the estimated error is about 5%, but with our experience and BIM capabilities, we have designed the aluminum formwork for the above works with the error more or less 2%. Particularly, with The Sun Avenue Project we reached an accuracy of no more than 10/14000 components.
